Archived Data

We store Discord IDs, Economy Fragments, and Reputation points to ensure the continuity of the performance. This data is essential for the bot's systems to recognize your status within the multiverse.

No puppet data is sold to external kingdoms; it belongs solely to the Archive and is used only to maintain your theatrical standing.

Dossier Erasure

Dossier wipes and total data removal can be requested at any time within the Domain (Discord Support). Once your request is processed, your strings are forever cut from our history and all associated records are purged from the vault.

Cookies & Storage

The Archive uses local storage only to remember your session and preferences. We do not track your movements outside of our theater walls.
